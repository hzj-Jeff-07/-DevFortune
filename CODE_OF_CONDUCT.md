# 行为准则 / Code of Conduct

## 贡献者公约 / Contributor Covenant

本行为准则改编自 [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)，并针对 DevFortune 项目的独特性质进行了定制。DevFortune 融合了中国传统文化（天干地支、五行）与现代软件工程，因此我们对文化尊重有额外的要求。

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), customized for the DevFortune project. DevFortune bridges traditional Chinese cultural knowledge (TianGan-DiZhi, WuXing) with modern software engineering, so we hold additional expectations around cultural respect.

---

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

作为成员、贡献者和领导者，我们承诺让参与我们社区的每个人都不受骚扰，无论其年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、民族、性别特征、性别认同和表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向如何。

我们承诺以有助于建设一个开放、友善、多元、包容和健康的社区的方式行事和互动。

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## 我们的标准 / Our Standards

### 有助于营造积极环境的行为 / Positive Behaviors

- **展现同理心和善意** / Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
- **尊重不同的观点和经验** / Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **给予和优雅地接受建设性反馈** / Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- **承担责任并向受影响的人道歉，从经验中学习** / Accepting responsibility, apologizing to those affected, and learning from the experience
- **关注社区整体的最大利益** / Focusing on what is best for the overall community

### 尊重传统文化知识 / Respect for Traditional Cultural Knowledge

DevFortune 项目涉及中国传统文化中的天干地支和五行理论。我们要求所有参与者：

DevFortune incorporates traditional Chinese cultural knowledge around TianGan-DiZhi and WuXing theory. We ask all participants to:

- **以尊重的态度对待传统文化知识**：天干地支和五行理论拥有数千年的历史，应当被视为有价值的文化遗产，即使你不认同其哲学基础。
  Treat traditional cultural knowledge with respect. TianGan-DiZhi and WuXing theory carry thousands of years of history and should be regarded as valuable cultural heritage, even if you do not share their philosophical foundations.

- **避免对传统文化进行贬低或嘲讽**：本项目以创造性和趣味性的方式将传统理论映射到编程活动中，但这不意味着可以嘲笑或贬低这些文化传统本身。
  Avoid demeaning or mocking traditional culture. This project maps traditional theory to programming activities in creative and playful ways, but this does not open the door to ridiculing or belittling the cultural traditions themselves.

- **欢迎对传统知识的严谨讨论**：我们鼓励关于如何更准确地表达和应用传统理论的建设性讨论。
  Welcome rigorous discussion of traditional knowledge. We encourage constructive conversations about how to represent and apply traditional theory more accurately.

- **承认文化知识的多样性**：对传统理论的理解和诠释可能因地区、流派而异，请尊重这种多样性。
  Acknowledge the diversity within cultural knowledge. Understandings and interpretations of traditional theory may vary by region and school of thought; please respect this diversity.

### 不可接受的行为 / Unacceptable Behaviors

- **使用性暗示的语言或图像，以及任何形式的性关注或挑逗** / The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- **挑衅、侮辱或贬损性评论，以及人身或政治攻击** / Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- **公开或私下的骚扰** / Public or private harassment
- **未经明确许可，发布他人的私人信息** / Publishing others' private information without their explicit permission
- **对传统文化知识进行恶意歪曲或故意亵渎** / Malicious distortion or deliberate desecration of traditional cultural knowledge
- **其他在专业环境中可被合理认为不当的行为** / Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 执行责任 / Enforcement Responsibilities

社区领导者负责澄清和执行我们的可接受行为标准，并在他们认为任何行为不当、有威胁性、冒犯性或有害时，采取适当且公平的纠正措施。

社区领导者有权利和责任删除、编辑或拒绝不符合本行为准则的评论、提交、代码、Wiki 编辑、Issue 和其他贡献，并在适当时传达审核决定的原因。

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## 适用范围 / Scope

本行为准则适用于所有社区空间，也适用于个人在公共空间代表社区时的行为。代表社区的例子包括使用官方电子邮件地址、通过官方社交媒体帐户发布信息或在线上或线下活动中担任指定代表。

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

---

## 执行 / Enforcement

如果你遇到辱骂、骚扰或其他不可接受的行为，请通过以下方式向负责执行的社区领导者报告：

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at:

- **GitHub Issues**：在项目仓库中提交标记为 `conduct` 的 Issue / Open an issue in the repository labeled `conduct`
- **电子邮件 / Email**：[conduct@devfortune.dev](mailto:conduct@devfortune.dev)

所有投诉都将被及时、公平地审查和调查。所有社区领导者都有义务尊重事件报告者的隐私和安全。

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## 执行指南 / Enforcement Guidelines

社区领导者将遵循以下社区影响指南来确定针对任何违反本行为准则的行为所采取的后果：

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. 纠正 / Correction

**社区影响 / Community Impact**：使用不当语言或其他被认为不专业或在社区中不受欢迎的行为。

Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome in the community.

**后果 / Consequence**：社区领导者发出私下书面警告，明确说明违规行为的性质以及为什么该行为不当。可能会要求公开道歉。

A private, written warning from community leaders, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. 警告 / Warning

**社区影响 / Community Impact**：通过单个事件或一系列行为造成的违规。

A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**后果 / Consequence**：对持续行为的警告及后果说明。在指定时间内不得与相关人员互动，包括不得在社区空间和社交媒体等外部渠道中进行主动互动。违反这些条款可能导致临时或永久封禁。

A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved for a specified period of time, including unsolicited interaction in community spaces and external channels like social media. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. 临时封禁 / Temporary Ban

**社区影响 / Community Impact**：严重违反社区标准，包括持续的不当行为。

A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**后果 / Consequence**：在指定时间内临时禁止与社区进行任何形式的互动或公开交流。在此期间，不得与相关人员进行任何公开或私下互动，包括不得在社区空间中进行主动互动。违反这些条款可能导致永久封禁。

A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the people involved is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. 永久封禁 / Permanent Ban

**社区影响 / Community Impact**：表现出违反社区标准的模式，包括持续的不当行为、对个人的骚扰或对群体的攻击或贬低。

Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.

**后果 / Consequence**：永久禁止在社区内进行任何形式的公开互动。

A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
